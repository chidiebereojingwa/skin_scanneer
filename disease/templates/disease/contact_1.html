About us

The Mission

Since our founding in 2020, MySkinDoc has been the go-to skin cancer detection service for countless people. MySkinDoc is a platform where the user can upload the picture of the spot on the skin and our trained model would classify the cancer as either high or low risk. Users can also compare and track any skin changes over time through our MyJounal feature.

Process

This application uses Image processing, Deep Learning and Google Cloud functionalities to process the input image, calssify it and predict the health condition

Future plans

  • Creating a mobile application to increase the reach of application.
  • Collect more data to tune the performance of Machine learning model.
  • Collaborating with hospitals and connecting users to respective hospitals based on user health condition
